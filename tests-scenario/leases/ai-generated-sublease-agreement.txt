SUBLEASE AGREEMENT

THIS SUBLEASE AGREEMENT ("Sublease") is made and entered into as of the 20th day of
February, 2026, by and between:

SUBLANDLORD: Vertex Analytics, Inc., a Massachusetts corporation, with offices at
One Federal Street, 25th Floor, Boston, MA 02110 ("Sublandlord")

SUBTENANT: Calloway Data Systems, LLC, a Massachusetts limited liability company, with
offices at 150 Cambridge Park Drive, Suite 200, Cambridge, MA 02140 ("Subtenant")

RECITALS

A. Sublandlord is the tenant under that certain Office Lease Agreement dated September 1,
2022 (the "Master Lease"), between Harbor Point Realty Trust ("Master Landlord") and
Sublandlord, covering approximately 15,000 rentable square feet on the 25th floor of One
Federal Street, Boston, MA 02110 (the "Master Premises").

B. Sublandlord desires to sublease a portion of the Master Premises to Subtenant, and
Subtenant desires to sublease such space from Sublandlord.

C. The Master Lease permits subletting with Master Landlord's prior written consent, which
consent shall not be unreasonably withheld.

AGREEMENT

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree:

ARTICLE 1. SUBLEASE PREMISES

1.1 Premises. Sublandlord subleases to Subtenant approximately 6,200 rentable square feet
of office space constituting the eastern portion of the 25th floor, as depicted on Exhibit
A attached hereto (the "Sublease Premises"). The Sublease Premises include access to six
(6) private offices, an open workspace area, a kitchenette, and a small conference room.

1.2 Shared Facilities. Subtenant shall have non-exclusive access to the main conference
room (Suite 2510), the reception area, and the kitchen facilities on the 25th floor,
subject to reasonable scheduling and use policies established by Sublandlord.

1.3 Furniture. Sublandlord shall provide the Sublease Premises furnished with existing
office furniture including desks, chairs, filing cabinets, and conference table. Subtenant
shall maintain such furniture in good condition and shall not remove it from the Premises.

ARTICLE 2. TERM

2.1 Sublease Term. The Sublease Term shall commence on April 15, 2026 ("Commencement
Date"), and shall expire on August 31, 2029, which is three (3) months prior to the
expiration of the Master Lease on November 30, 2029.

2.2 Early Termination. Subtenant may terminate this Sublease upon one hundred twenty (120)
days' written notice at any time after the first anniversary of the Commencement Date,
subject to payment of an early termination fee equal to four (4) months of Sublease Rent.

2.3 Master Lease Termination. This Sublease is subject and subordinate to the Master
Lease. If the Master Lease terminates for any reason prior to the expiration of the
Sublease Term, this Sublease shall simultaneously terminate, and Sublandlord shall have
no liability to Subtenant on account of such termination, except to refund any prepaid
rent and the security deposit.

ARTICLE 3. RENT

3.1 Sublease Rent. Subtenant shall pay Sublandlord monthly rent as follows:

    Months 1-18:    $58.00/RSF/year ($359,600.00/year; $29,966.67/month)
    Months 19-36:   $60.00/RSF/year ($372,000.00/year; $31,000.00/month)
    Months 37-41:   $62.00/RSF/year ($384,400.00/year; $32,033.33/month)

3.2 Additional Charges. Subtenant shall reimburse Sublandlord for Subtenant's proportionate
share (41.33%, based on 6,200/15,000 RSF) of any increases in Operating Expenses and Taxes
payable by Sublandlord under the Master Lease over the base year amounts established
therein.

3.3 Payment. Sublease Rent shall be due on the first (1st) day of each calendar month,
payable by check or electronic transfer to the account designated by Sublandlord. Rent for
any partial month shall be prorated on a per diem basis.

3.4 Late Payment. A late fee of three percent (3%) of the overdue amount shall be charged
on any payment received more than seven (7) days after the due date.

ARTICLE 4. SECURITY DEPOSIT

4.1 Deposit. Upon execution, Subtenant shall deliver to Sublandlord a security deposit of
$62,000.00, equal to approximately two (2) months of initial Sublease Rent.

4.2 Application and Return. The security deposit may be applied to any Subtenant default.
The balance shall be returned within thirty (30) days after the expiration of the Sublease
Term and Subtenant's surrender of the Sublease Premises, less any lawful deductions.

ARTICLE 5. MASTER LEASE INCORPORATION

5.1 Incorporation by Reference. Except as otherwise provided herein, the terms and
conditions of the Master Lease are incorporated herein by reference as if fully set forth,
and Subtenant shall observe and perform all obligations of the "Tenant" under the Master
Lease with respect to the Sublease Premises.

5.2 Exceptions. The following provisions of the Master Lease shall NOT apply to this
Sublease: (a) any renewal or extension options; (b) any expansion rights or rights of
first refusal; (c) any tenant improvement allowance; (d) any free rent periods; (e) any
provisions regarding the initial build-out of the Master Premises.

5.3 Conflicts. In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Sublease and the
Master Lease, the terms of this Sublease shall control as between Sublandlord and
Subtenant, provided that nothing herein shall be construed to modify or amend the Master
Lease or Sublandlord's obligations thereunder.

ARTICLE 6. MASTER LANDLORD CONSENT

6.1 Condition Precedent. This Sublease is expressly conditioned upon Sublandlord's
obtaining the written consent of Master Landlord to this Sublease within forty-five (45)
days after the date hereof. If such consent is not obtained within said period, either
party may terminate this Sublease by written notice to the other, and Sublandlord shall
promptly refund to Subtenant any amounts previously paid.

6.2 Sublandlord's Obligation. Sublandlord shall use commercially reasonable efforts to
obtain Master Landlord's consent promptly.

ARTICLE 7. USE

7.1 Permitted Use. The Sublease Premises shall be used solely for general office purposes,
including data analytics, software development, and related professional services.

7.2 Compliance. Subtenant shall comply with all applicable laws and with all rules and
regulations of the Building as set forth in the Master Lease.

ARTICLE 8. MAINTENANCE AND SERVICES

8.1 Building Services. Sublandlord shall pass through to Subtenant all building services
that Sublandlord receives under the Master Lease, including janitorial service, HVAC,
elevator service, and common area maintenance. Sublandlord shall not be liable for any
interruption or inadequacy of such services.

8.2 Subtenant's Obligations. Subtenant shall maintain the interior of the Sublease
Premises in clean and good condition. Subtenant shall be responsible for any damage caused
by Subtenant, its employees, or invitees beyond normal wear and tear.

8.3 IT Infrastructure. Subtenant shall be responsible for its own telecommunications, data
cabling, and IT infrastructure within the Sublease Premises. Any cabling installations
must be coordinated with Building management.

ARTICLE 9. ALTERATIONS

9.1 No Alterations. Subtenant shall not make any alterations, additions, or improvements
to the Sublease Premises without the prior written consent of both Sublandlord and Master
Landlord.

9.2 Restoration. Upon expiration or termination, Subtenant shall, at its sole cost,
restore the Sublease Premises to their condition as of the Commencement Date, reasonable
wear and tear excepted, unless Sublandlord waives such requirement in writing.

ARTICLE 10. INSURANCE

10.1 Subtenant's Insurance. Subtenant shall maintain: (a) commercial general liability
insurance, $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate; (b) property insurance for
Subtenant's personal property; (c) workers' compensation as required by law.

10.2 Additional Insureds. All liability policies shall name Sublandlord, Master Landlord,
and their respective agents as additional insureds.

10.3 Certificates. Subtenant shall deliver certificates of insurance to Sublandlord prior
to the Commencement Date and upon each renewal.

ARTICLE 11. ASSIGNMENT AND FURTHER SUBLETTING

11.1 Prohibition. Subtenant shall not assign this Sublease or further sublet the Sublease
Premises or any portion thereof without the prior written consent of both Sublandlord and
Master Landlord.

ARTICLE 12. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

12.1 Subtenant Default. Each of the following constitutes an event of default: (a) failure
to pay Rent within five (5) days after written notice; (b) breach of any other covenant
not cured within fifteen (15) days after written notice; (c) bankruptcy or insolvency.

12.2 Sublandlord's Remedies. Upon default, Sublandlord may: (a) terminate this Sublease
upon five (5) days' notice; (b) re-enter and repossess the Sublease Premises; (c) recover
all damages including accelerated rent and costs of reletting.

[ANOMALY: Temporal impossibility -- Section 12.1(b) allows 15 days to cure a non-monetary
default, but Section 12.2(a) allows termination on only 5 days' notice. The cure period
should be longer than the termination notice period for it to be meaningful.]

12.3 Sublandlord Default. If Sublandlord defaults in its obligations, Subtenant shall give
written notice, and Sublandlord shall have thirty (30) days to cure. If Sublandlord fails
to cure, Subtenant may pursue remedies at law or equity, but shall not have the right to
terminate this Sublease except in the case of a material breach that renders the Sublease
Premises unusable.

ARTICLE 13. INDEMNIFICATION

13.1 Subtenant's Indemnification. Subtenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Sublandlord
from all claims arising from Subtenant's use of the Sublease Premises or Subtenant's
breach of this Sublease.

13.2 Sublandlord's Indemnification. Sublandlord shall indemnify and hold harmless
Subtenant from claims arising from Sublandlord's breach of this Sublease or the Master
Lease to the extent such breach adversely affects Subtenant.

ARTICLE 14. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

14.1 Sublandlord Representations. Sublandlord represents and warrants that: (a) the
Master Lease is in full force and effect; (b) Sublandlord is not in default under the
Master Lease; (c) the Master Lease permits subletting with Master Landlord's consent;
(d) a true and complete copy of the Master Lease has been provided to Subtenant.

14.2 Subtenant Representations. Subtenant represents and warrants that it has reviewed
the Master Lease and is familiar with its terms and conditions.

ARTICLE 15. MISCELLANEOUS

15.1 Governing Law. This Sublease shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of
Massachusetts.

15.2 Dispute Resolution. Any dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration in Boston,
Massachusetts, under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The prevailing
party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

15.3 Entire Agreement. This Sublease, together with the Master Lease as incorporated
herein, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

15.4 Notices. All notices shall be in writing and sent by certified mail or overnight
courier to the addresses first set forth above.

15.5 Broker. The parties acknowledge that Newmark (representing Sublandlord) and Cushman
& Wakefield (representing Subtenant) are the only brokers involved. Sublandlord shall be
responsible for all brokerage commissions.

15.6 Counterparts. This Sublease may be executed in counterparts.
