EQUIPMENT LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS EQUIPMENT LEASE AGREEMENT ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of the 1st day
of April, 2026, by and between:

LESSOR: Titan Capital Equipment Leasing Corporation, a Delaware corporation, with its
principal office at 2000 Market Street, Suite 2800, Philadelphia, PA 19103 ("Lessor")

LESSEE: Appalachian Mining & Excavation, LLC, a West Virginia limited liability company,
with its principal office at 500 Virginia Street East, Suite 1200, Charleston, WV 25301
("Lessee")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease certain heavy construction and mining equipment for use
in its excavation and mining operations; and

WHEREAS, Lessor is in the business of leasing heavy equipment and is willing to lease such
equipment to Lessee upon the terms and conditions set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein, and for
good and valuable consideration, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1. EQUIPMENT

1.1 Leased Equipment. Lessor hereby leases to Lessee, and Lessee hereby leases from
Lessor, the following equipment (collectively, the "Equipment"):

    Item 1: Caterpillar 390F Hydraulic Excavator
             Serial No.: CAT0390FKJM04872
             Year: 2025
             Estimated Fair Market Value: $625,000.00

    Item 2: Komatsu PC490LC-11 Hydraulic Excavator
             Serial No.: KMT490LCA7P02195
             Year: 2024
             Estimated Fair Market Value: $580,000.00

    Item 3: Caterpillar D8T Dozer
             Serial No.: CAT0D8T0MBY03614
             Year: 2025
             Estimated Fair Market Value: $485,000.00

    Item 4: Volvo A45G Articulated Hauler
             Serial No.: VOL0A45GKNH01847
             Year: 2025
             Estimated Fair Market Value: $410,000.00

    Total Estimated Fair Market Value of Equipment: $2,100,000.00

1.2 Delivery. Lessor shall deliver the Equipment to Lessee's designated work site at
1400 Coal River Road, Whitesville, WV 25209, on or before April 15, 2026. Risk of loss
shall transfer to Lessee upon delivery.

1.3 Acceptance. Lessee shall inspect the Equipment within five (5) business days of
delivery and shall execute an Acceptance Certificate (Exhibit B) confirming that the
Equipment is in good working order and acceptable condition. If Lessee fails to deliver
a written rejection within the five-day inspection period, the Equipment shall be deemed
accepted.

ARTICLE 2. LEASE TERM

2.1 Initial Term. The initial lease term ("Initial Term") shall be forty-eight (48) months,
commencing on the date of Lessee's acceptance of the Equipment (the "Commencement Date")
and expiring forty-eight (48) months thereafter.

2.2 Renewal. Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Lease shall automatically renew for
successive twelve (12) month periods under the same terms and conditions, unless either
party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end
of the then-current term.

2.3 Early Termination. Lessee may not terminate this Lease during the Initial Term. In the
event of early termination by Lessee during the Initial Term, Lessee shall pay Lessor a
termination fee equal to the sum of all remaining Lease Payments for the balance of the
Initial Term, discounted at a rate of three percent (3%).

ARTICLE 3. LEASE PAYMENTS

3.1 Monthly Payments. Lessee shall pay Lessor monthly lease payments ("Lease Payments")
of Forty-Seven Thousand Eight Hundred Dollars ($47,800.00), payable in advance on the
first (1st) day of each month during the Lease Term.

3.2 Total Lease Cost. The total base lease cost over the forty-eight (48) month Initial
Term shall be Two Million Two Hundred Ninety-Four Thousand Four Hundred Dollars
($2,294,400.00).

3.3 Security Deposit. Upon execution of this Agreement, Lessee shall pay Lessor a security
deposit of Ninety-Five Thousand Six Hundred Dollars ($95,600.00), equal to two (2) months
of Lease Payments. The security deposit shall be returned within sixty (60) days after the
expiration of the Lease Term and the return of the Equipment in acceptable condition.

3.4 Payment Method. All payments shall be made by wire transfer to Lessor's designated
bank account. Payments not received within five (5) business days of the due date shall
incur a late fee of two percent (2%) per month on the outstanding balance.

3.5 Net Lease. This is a net lease. All Lease Payments shall be made without deduction,
setoff, or counterclaim. Lessee shall be responsible for all costs associated with the
operation, maintenance, repair, insurance, taxes, and licensing of the Equipment.

ARTICLE 4. USE OF EQUIPMENT

4.1 Permitted Use. The Equipment shall be used solely for surface mining, excavation,
grading, and related earth-moving operations within the State of West Virginia.

4.2 Location. The Equipment shall be maintained at the work site specified in Section 1.2
or at such other location within West Virginia as Lessee may designate with prior written
notice to Lessor. The Equipment shall not be removed from the State of West Virginia
without Lessor's prior written consent.

4.3 Operator Qualifications. The Equipment shall be operated only by qualified, properly
trained, and licensed personnel.

4.4 Compliance. Lessee shall operate the Equipment in compliance with all applicable
federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and safety standards, including MSHA (Mine
Safety and Health Administration) regulations.

4.5 Hour Limitations. The Equipment shall not exceed the following annual operating hour
limits without Lessor's prior consent and payment of additional usage charges:

    Excavators (Items 1 & 2):  2,500 hours per year
    Dozer (Item 3):            2,200 hours per year
    Hauler (Item 4):           2,800 hours per year

    Excess usage rate: $85.00 per hour for excavators; $75.00 per hour for dozers;
    $65.00 per hour for haulers.

ARTICLE 5. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIR

5.1 Lessee's Obligations. Lessee shall, at Lessee's sole cost and expense:

    (a) Maintain the Equipment in good working order, condition, and repair in accordance
        with the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedules;
    (b) Use only manufacturer-approved parts, fluids, and lubricants;
    (c) Keep accurate maintenance logs and hour meter records for each item of Equipment;
    (d) Make all maintenance logs available for inspection by Lessor upon request;
    (e) Perform all required safety inspections and maintain MSHA compliance records;
    (f) Promptly repair any damage or malfunction, using authorized service providers.

5.2 Major Repairs. Any single repair estimated to cost in excess of Twenty-Five Thousand
Dollars ($25,000.00) shall require Lessor's prior written approval. Lessee shall obtain at
least two (2) competitive repair estimates for any such major repair.

5.3 Modifications. Lessee shall not make any modifications, alterations, or additions to
the Equipment without Lessor's prior written consent. Any approved modifications shall
become the property of Lessor.

ARTICLE 6. INSURANCE

6.1 Required Coverage. Lessee shall maintain, at Lessee's sole expense, the following
insurance coverages for the full Lease Term:

    (a) All-risk property insurance covering the Equipment at full replacement value
        (not less than $2,100,000.00 in the aggregate);
    (b) Commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000
        per occurrence and $5,000,000 aggregate;
    (c) Automobile liability insurance, $1,000,000 combined single limit;
    (d) Workers' compensation as required by West Virginia law;
    (e) Umbrella/excess liability, $5,000,000.

6.2 Lessor as Loss Payee and Additional Insured. All property insurance policies shall
name Lessor as loss payee. All liability policies shall name Lessor as additional insured.

6.3 Insurance Certificates. Lessee shall provide Lessor with certificates of insurance
prior to the Commencement Date and annually thereafter, or upon any policy renewal.
Policies shall require thirty (30) days' prior written notice to Lessor of cancellation
or material change.

ARTICLE 7. TITLE AND OWNERSHIP

7.1 Lessor's Title. The Equipment is and shall remain the sole property of Lessor. Lessee
acquires no right, title, or interest in the Equipment except the right of use as provided
herein.

7.2 Financing Statements. Lessee authorizes Lessor to file UCC financing statements and
any other documents necessary to perfect Lessor's interest in the Equipment.

7.3 No Encumbrances. Lessee shall not pledge, mortgage, encumber, or permit any lien to
be placed on the Equipment.

7.4 Identification. Lessee shall not remove, alter, or obscure any serial numbers,
manufacturer's plates, or Lessor's identification labels on the Equipment.

ARTICLE 8. PURCHASE OPTION

8.1 Fair Market Value Purchase Option. Upon expiration of the Initial Term, provided
Lessee is not in default, Lessee shall have the option to purchase any or all items of
Equipment at their then-fair market value, as determined by an independent appraiser
mutually agreed upon by the parties.

8.2 Fixed Purchase Option. Alternatively, Lessee may purchase the Equipment at the end
of the Initial Term at the following fixed prices:

    Item 1 (CAT 390F):     $156,250.00 (25% of original FMV)
    Item 2 (Komatsu PC490): $145,000.00 (25% of original FMV)
    Item 3 (CAT D8T):      $121,250.00 (25% of original FMV)
    Item 4 (Volvo A45G):   $102,500.00 (25% of original FMV)

8.3 Exercise of Option. Lessee must provide written notice of its intent to exercise a
purchase option at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the Initial Term.

ARTICLE 9. RETURN OF EQUIPMENT

9.1 Condition. Upon expiration or termination and if Lessee does not exercise a purchase
option, Lessee shall return the Equipment to Lessor's designated location in good working
condition, ordinary wear and tear excepted, at Lessee's sole cost and expense.

9.2 Return Standards. The Equipment shall be returned:
    (a) Clean and free of debris;
    (b) With all fluids at proper levels;
    (c) With all maintenance current per manufacturer's schedule;
    (d) With all original and replacement parts intact;
    (e) Free of any damage beyond normal wear;
    (f) With all maintenance records and hour meter documentation.

9.3 Inspection. Lessor shall inspect the Equipment within ten (10) business days of
return. Any deficiencies beyond normal wear and tear shall be repaired at Lessee's expense
or charged against the security deposit.

ARTICLE 10. LOSS OR DAMAGE

10.1 Risk of Loss. Lessee bears all risk of loss, theft, damage, or destruction of the
Equipment from the date of delivery until the Equipment is returned to Lessor.

10.2 Total Loss. In the event of a total loss or constructive total loss of any item of
Equipment, Lessee shall: (a) promptly notify Lessor; (b) pay Lessor the stipulated loss
value set forth in Exhibit C; and (c) assign all insurance proceeds to Lessor.

10.3 Partial Loss. In the event of damage short of a total loss, Lessee shall promptly
repair the Equipment to its pre-damage condition at Lessee's sole expense.

ARTICLE 11. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

11.1 Events of Default. The following shall constitute events of default:
    (a) Failure to make any Lease Payment within ten (10) days of the due date;
    (b) Breach of any material covenant not cured within thirty (30) days after notice;
    (c) Filing of a petition in bankruptcy by or against Lessee;
    (d) Material misrepresentation by Lessee;
    (e) Failure to maintain required insurance;
    (f) Unauthorized relocation or encumbrance of the Equipment.

11.2 Remedies. Upon default, Lessor may: (a) terminate this Lease; (b) take immediate
possession of the Equipment without judicial process to the extent permitted by law;
(c) recover all unpaid Lease Payments for the balance of the term; (d) recover damages
including costs of repossession, repair, and re-leasing; (e) pursue any other remedy.

11.3 Lessee's Obligations Upon Default. Upon any event of default, Lessee shall make the
Equipment available for repossession at the location specified in Section 1.2 or such
other location as Lessor may designate.

ARTICLE 12. INDEMNIFICATION

12.1 Lessee shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lessor from and against any and all
claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys'
fees) arising out of or relating to: (a) Lessee's possession, use, maintenance, or
operation of the Equipment; (b) any injury to persons or property caused by or related to
the Equipment; (c) any environmental contamination related to the Equipment. This
indemnification shall survive the expiration or termination of this Lease.

ARTICLE 13. TAXES

13.1 Lessee shall pay when due all sales, use, personal property, excise, and other taxes,
fees, and assessments levied on the Equipment or on Lessee's use thereof, excluding only
Lessor's federal and state income taxes.

ARTICLE 14. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

14.1 Lessee represents that: (a) it is duly organized and in good standing; (b) it has
authority to enter this Agreement; (c) its financial statements are accurate; (d) there is
no litigation pending that would materially affect its ability to perform.

14.2 LESSOR MAKES NO WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF
MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE. LESSEE ACCEPTS THE EQUIPMENT
"AS-IS" UPON ACCEPTANCE.

ARTICLE 15. MISCELLANEOUS

15.1 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

15.2 Dispute Resolution. Any dispute shall be resolved in the state or federal courts
located in New Castle County, Delaware. Each party consents to personal jurisdiction in
such courts.

15.3 Entire Agreement. This Agreement, including all exhibits, constitutes the entire
agreement between the parties.

15.4 Amendments. No amendment shall be effective unless in writing signed by both parties.

15.5 Assignment by Lessor. Lessor may assign this Lease or its rights hereunder without
Lessee's consent. Lessee may not assign this Lease without Lessor's prior written consent.

15.6 Notices. All notices shall be in writing, sent by certified mail or overnight courier.

15.7 Severability. Invalid provisions shall be severed without affecting remaining
provisions.

15.8 Waiver. No waiver of any default shall be deemed a waiver of any subsequent default.

15.9 Headings. Section headings are for convenience only.
